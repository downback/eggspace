Burning the Midnight Oil
11.02.22-25.02.22
Richard Janecek and Sebastian Eduardo
Text by Tobias Bärtsch
Burning the Midnight Oil features work by Prague-based artist Richard Janecek and ZHdK BA alumni Sebastian Eduardo and centers around themes of escapism and digital realities. Janecek presents a video work featuring a conversation between three of the world’s most popular chatbots while the camera circles an eternal synthetic bonfire. Eduardo shows a continuation of his work on small screens with two new works that investigate the desire to move from this world into an alternate realm.

Richard Janeček (*1990) is a Czech intermedia artist who graduated from the Studio of Photography and New Media at FAMU in Prague. He is focused on the image found in the late period of the photographic method moving smoothly into the field of virtual culture.

Tobias Bärtsch (b. 1993 in Walenstadt, Switzerland) is a Swiss-Polish artist and writer living and working in Zurich, Switzerland.
