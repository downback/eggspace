Night-blooming
04.03.22-18.03.22
Jana Kohler and Richard Janecek
Text by Gregor Vogel
Egg presents Night-blooming, featuring work from ZHdK alumna Jana Kohler and Prague-based artist Richard Janecek examining fallacies in the realms of love and technology. Kohler, in collaboration with Rémy Sax, performs a variation of the country song, Never Promised You a Rose Garden, to investigate tropes and illusions of romance. Janecek’s video work also follows the theme of misconceptions with a fictionalized narrative created in collaboration with a writing AI on the conspiracy theories surrounding the 5G network.

Jana Kohler (*1998, St. Gallen, CH) completed her Fine Arts studies at ZHdK in 2021 and has since been working as an artist and bookseller. Using the medium of video, she explores characters that wander through dream worlds and constantly reinvent themselves. The works relate to cinéma through their visual appearance and attempt to find a new language for the familiar and break up genres. Selfbuilt sets and amateur actors are always involved in the productions. She is also part of the band "Bingo Hall River Boys" and works as a DJ.

Richard Janeček (*1990) is a Czech intermedia artist who graduated from the Studio of Photography and New Media at FAMU in Prague. He is focused on the image found in the late period of the photographic method moving smoothly into the field of virtual culture.

Gregor Vogel is a Zürich artist who recently escaped to New Zealand.
