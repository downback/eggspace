A Phantasmagorical Bloom
01.04.22- 11.04.22
Hanna Somoson and Jana Kohler
Text by Isa Lumme
A Phantasmagorical Bloom blends fantasy and shades of reality with a tinge of the uncanny, inviting us to enter the half-life between waking and sleeping. Estonian-born artist Hanna Samoson exhibits her video 2222, which follows her trek across the surreal and glacial landscapes of the islands of Estonia in winter. As she is accompanied by a demon-faced companion, the landscape becomes populated with strange, arcane happenings. ZHdK alumna Jana Kohler shows a selection of clips from her oeuvre which reveal the central themes in her creative universe. The exploration of femininity recurs, always through the highly aesthetic lens of fiction.

Peilian Li is a Swiss-Chinese visual artist, performer and writer. Her works forgo ordinary logic in favor of an onirical elation of ideas that often touch the identity issues of being an Asian immigrant in the Western world. She graduated with a Master in visual arts at ECAL / Ecole Cantonale d’Art de Lausanne in 2022 and she currently lives and works in Lausanne, Switzerland.

César Axel (*1992) is a Mexican visual and multimedia artist living and working in Lausanne. His work is informed by fields diverse as art theory, philosophy, political economy, and technology studies, allowing for a radical expansion of the core concerns of art’s social scope, or, the role it can play in the transformation of our political environment, our effects, as well as technologically-mediated subjectivities. César Axel has participated in exhibitions in Switzerland, USA, Mexico, Bucharest and China.
